"Close to 800,000 people die due to suicide every year. Suicide is the second leading cause of death in 15-29-year-olds."

Cited from the World Health Organization in early 2020, this quote represents the deadly dangers of how poor mental health can affect individuals all around the world. Especially during the global pandemic in these times, many people are prone to these detrimental influences.

Passionate about stopping these tragic impacts, we, a team of four students from California Polytechnic State University at San Luis Obispo, seek to provide a space of comfort to anyone struggling with their mental state.

What Does AssessMental Do?

AssessMental provides countless resources that attempt to ease the state of mind, such as calming audios, in-depth sources that discuss mental health, therapy videos, infographics, and tips for combatting negative thoughts.

Questioning if you need help?

Take the test on AssessMental to find out whether you should be considering reaching out to professionals.
